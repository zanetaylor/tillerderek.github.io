Our Story

Your Style Is Our Brand

In 2007, our Founders Meredith and William opened their doors. From day one, the goal of Brand Salon was to bring the latest trends in hair design, to the people of Asheville.

All of our stylists are trained in the latest techniques, and we only use professional hair products with our clients. Rest assured, your Brand is in good hands.
